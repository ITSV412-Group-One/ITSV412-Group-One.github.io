EHRD 391 Class Review!

Mel's Review:

Professor Judy Sandlin, gives lecture in every class to prepare you for every upcoming exam. It is pretty easy to stay on track as long as you attend class and do the homework each time you learn the material.

The best way to succeed in this class is to take notes for each lecture and follow along with the homework and practices. Going to office hours will help you greatly if you have lost track and are struggling.

Overall, I would rate this class a 8/10.

Danny's Review:

Professor Sandlin's course on Measurement and Evaluation in Human Resource Development and Technology Mangement is a rigorous, yet rewarding course.

While participating in the course, my routine involved attending lectures and revisiting my class notes before working on my homework assignments. Engaging in collaborative efforts with my classmates was vital in gaining a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter. Additionally, I agree with Mel's suggestion of utilizing Office Hours, especially when encountering particularly challenging concepts

Overall I would rate Professor Sandlin's course a 8/10.
