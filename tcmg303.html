TCMG 303 Class Review!

Andres' Review:

Dr. Walker's class has been my favorite class so far in the TCMG/ITSV program. His enthusiasm for the subject shines through and makes it fun to listen to his lectures.

Taking his class motivated me learn more about Linux and even install it on my laptop.

There isn't anything I would change from the class - from the way the lectures and labs are handled to the grading policies. Overall, I would rate this class a 10/10!
